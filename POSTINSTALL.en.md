# Content Studio is ready

Plan your content calendar from a simple monthly view: create posts, assign
dates, channels, categories, colors, captions, and image or video structure.

You can start by:

- Creating categories for pillars, campaigns, or formats.
- Adding posts directly from the calendar.
- Dragging posts between days to adjust your plan.
- Attaching files or references to each post.
- Using Planner to ask the Agent for weekly content ideas, tone adjustments, or
  structured post drafts.

Everything stays local inside the app private workspace.
